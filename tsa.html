Seven Lakes High School TSA

What is TSA?

The TSA, or Technology Student Association, is a chapter-based organization that gives middle and high school students the chance to participate in competitions related to technology.

Competitions available to students focus on a variety of categories, providing every student with a chance to pursue their interests. Examples of events include Coding, Tech Bowl, Children's Stories, and CAD Engineering, among many others.

Our Goals

Ultimately, we hope to encourage more SLHS students to pursue competitions in technology through our TSA chapter, encouraging others to seek technology-related fields and interests.

Over the next few years, we hope to grow our club and send more members to state and national events in all fields, both to promote the standing of our club and encourage others at SLHS to join.
