Providing high-quality medical care to a digital world.

Bringing the Doctors to You

TelePharma provides patients with access to high-quality medical care from the comfort of their homes, giving them the tools necessary to enjoy medicinal access digitally.

TelePharma users enjoy all the benefits of an in-person clinic through digital means, with patients having access to digital records, video and phone-based consultations, and a medical manager for prescriptions and treatments.

About Us

The Process

Medical Profile

Upon filling out a series of forms and questionnaires, our experts will generate a personalized medical profile containing various patient data points.

Consultations

Upon recieving a medical profile, patients can then schedule virtual appointments with TelePharma doctors, where they will recieve all the benefits of an in-person consultation.

Treatments

Patients will be provided with personalized treatments, prescriptions, and recovery routes, all managed through their TelePharma dashboard.

Combatting Coronavirus

We at TelePharma understand the devastating effects COVID-19 has had on the public. The world needs safe medical assistance now more than ever -- and we're here to help.

We have expanded TelePharma to include features that help patients protect themselves against COVID-19, giving them medical advice and easy access to healthcare in the safest way possible during these unprecedented times.
