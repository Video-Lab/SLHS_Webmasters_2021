Combatting COVID

Our Promise

We understand the devastating effect COVID-19 may have had on you and your health, and we are here to help.

We promise to continue providing affordable, safe, and high-quality medical care to our patients, protecting them from all illnesses, including COVID-19. In light of the pandemic, we have expanded our platform to include a range of features that help protect our patients from the Coronavirus.

TelePharma Coronavirus Protection

Test Administration

TelePharma gives patients the ability to schedule, manage, and monitor COVID-19 tests, with a vast amount of resources available to patients that test positive.

Quarantine & Symptom Management

TelePharma allows patients to track and share their symptoms- whether they have COVID-19 or not- with their assigned physicians, who will then provide them with care and assistance.

Vaccine Scheduling

TelePharma also allows patients to schedule and manage COVID-19 vaccine appointments, with ample access to vaccine resources and partnered testing facilities.
